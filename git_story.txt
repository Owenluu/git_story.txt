Harry Potter is known as the boy who lived. 
His parents were killed by Lord Voldemort during the First Wizarding War.
Volemort attempted to kill Harry but his spell backfired and it hit himself. 
Harry Potter lived because his mother protected him from the spell as a sacrifice. 
In the end Voldemort did not die from the spell backfiring. 
He survived because of contingencies he did to ensure his survival. 
Ultimately Harry Potter would grow up to be a powerful wizard. 
He and his close friends and allies took down Lord Voldemort and saved the wizarding world.
